What's New

  • School Playground and games period.

    Well,I just love this playground because in the games period the teacher brings us to the ground to play with our friends. Sometimes the teacher tells us to play a specific game,in which you have to sit and there will be a person with a hankerchief who will throw it behind you and then you have to take the hankerchief and run behind the person who threw it behind you and catch him before he sits. That's no fun, none of the students like to play that game. And sometimes in our games period the teacher tells us to play freestyle, which is so much fun because we can play with any of our friends , which all the students love to do. And sometimes in the games period our P.T. teacher takes us to the basement where there are carrom boards and snakes and ladders boards or chess boards. And then sends the 60% of the children to play carrom, 20% of the children to play chess and 20% of the children to play snakes and ladders.Its a lot of fun.

  • there should be a games period four times a week and games period teacher should take us to play regularly.

    Each and every student loves the games period because we get to play with our friends after our studies.
